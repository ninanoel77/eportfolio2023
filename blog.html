Sippin' through Tampa Bay: Java Joints Worth the Jive

By: Nina Noel

 

Hey there, my fellow caffeine addicts! If you're anything like me, your day doesn't officially kick off until you've had that first glorious sip of liquid gold - coffee, of course. And let me tell you, Tampa Bay knows how to brew up a storm. I have been in the heart of Tampa Florida for about four years now, and there is always a new coffee shop popping up in town. I don’t think I have ever tried a bad coffee here! Join me on a jittery journey through the best coffee shops in this sunny paradise, where the coffee is as bold as the Floridian sun.

 

Old School at Buddy Brew Coffee

 

Let's start with a classic, shall we? Buddy Brew Coffee is the OG of the Tampa coffee scene. Nestled in the heart of Hyde Park, this joint is like the granddaddy of all the cool kids in town. The exposed brick walls, the aroma of freshly roasted beans, and the hum of indie tunes in the background - it's a hipster's dream come true. The coffee shop has flooded Tampa Bay, with coffee so good that other coffee shops use their brew.

 

The baristas here aren't just slinging lattes; they're crafting liquid art. My personal favorite? The 'Buddy Brew Cold Brew,' a potion so potent it could wake the dead. If you're into Instagram-worthy moments, their latte art is impeccable. You can even ask for a scoop of vanilla gelato in your cold brew to create a cold brew float. Pro tip: snag a spot on their outdoor patio, soak up the Florida sunshine, and let the caffeine fuel your vibes. Also, make sure to add a fun seasonal flavor to your coffee for a little holiday cheer.

Café by Day, Bar by Night: King State

 

If you haven't experienced the magic of Cuban coffee, you're seriously missing out. Lucky for you, King State, in Seminole Heights has perfected the art of the cortado, and it's nothing short of wizardry. The moment you step inside, the scent of strong espresso hits you like a flavor freight train, in the best way possible.

 

Their 'Cafe Con Leche' is a silky, sweet symphony of espresso and steamed milk that'll transport you straight to the streets of Havana. The place has a laid-back, artsy vibe, with local artwork adorning the walls and mismatched furniture giving it that effortlessly cool feel. Don't be surprised if you lose track of time, sipping and people-watching in this caffeine haven.

 

Not to mention, at night, this place turns into a bar! You wouldn’t believe how good their espresso martini is…

The Oxford Exchange Elegance

 

Now, let's elevate our coffee experience. Enter the grandeur of the Oxford Exchange, where coffee meets sophistication. This place isn't just a coffee shop; it's an experience. The high ceilings, the intricate tile work, and the smell of luxury linger in the air.

 

Their espresso game is on a whole new level. The 'OE Macchiato' is a divine concoction of bold espresso with just a hint of velvety foam. Pair it with one of their decadent pastries, and you're in for a treat yo'self moment. Oxford Exchange isn't just a coffee shop; it's a destination for those who appreciate the finer things in life, caffeine included.

 

Try their Sunday brunch and try the best food EVER! Their portions are huge, and the loaded avocado toast is to die for. Not to mention, they also use Buddy Brew as their coffee brew!

The Hometown Hero: Blind Tiger Cafe

 

Tampa wouldn't be the same without its local legends, and Blind Tiger Cafe is our hometown hero. With multiple locations scattered across the city, Blind Tiger is like the Cheers of coffee joints - where everybody knows your name, or at least your coffee order.

 

The vibe here is eclectic, with exposed brick walls adorned with local art and a playlist that somehow always knows your mood. What makes Blind Tiger special is its commitment to community. The coffee is top-notch, but it's the sense of belonging that keeps you coming back. The 'Tiger Spice Latte' is a must-try, bringing together the comforting flavors of cinnamon and nutmeg in a warm embrace. This is a must-try in the fall.

 

They don’t just have coffee; they also have various lemonades and teas available. My favorite has got to be the lilac lemonade paired with a fun-flavored macaroon that they sell in-store.

Spaddy's Coffee Co.

 

Now, let's venture into the lesser-known. Spaddy's Coffee Co. is a hidden gem tucked away in Seminole Heights. I found this place after asking customers in King State what their next fave coffee spot is. This coffee caravan is not your typical brick-and-mortar affair; it's a coffee oasis in a repurposed 1962 Airstream.

 

The 'Spiced Cold Brew' is a revelation and sipping it while lounging in one of their vintage chairs feels like a scene from your favorite indie film. Can you tell I like cold brew yet? The folks here are friendly, the vibe is chill, and the coffee is as unique as the Airstream it's served from. If you're up for a caffeine adventure off the beaten path, Spaddy's is your destination.

 

The Lab Coffee

 

For those of us with a sweet tooth, The Lab Coffee in North Hyde Park is a sugary symphony for your taste buds. It's not just a coffee shop; it's a dessert destination. Picture this: a 'Caramel Crunch Latte' paired with a Nutella-stuffed croissant. Are you drooling yet?

 

This place is fairly new, but I discovered them quick because of their phenomenal location. Right by the University of Tampa and surrounded by three of the most popular apartment complexes in the area. If you’re a student at the University of Tampa, you’ve definitely passed this place before.

 

The Lab takes their coffee seriously, but they're not afraid to have a little fun with it. The 'Fruity Pebbles Cold Brew' is like a nostalgic trip to Saturday morning cartoons, but in coffee form. The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, with whimsical touches that make it feel like you're stepping into a mad scientist's coffee laboratory.

Sip Happens in Tampa Bay

 

In the grand scheme of things, Tampa Bay isn't just a coffee destination; it's a coffee revelation. From the hip vibes of Buddy Brew to the cultural fusion at King State, the elegance of Oxford Exchange, the local love at Blind Tiger, the hidden charm of Spaddy's, and the sweet symphony at The Lab - each coffee shop tells a unique story. And don’t worry, it doesn’t stop there. We have plenty more coffee shops and cafes. They are practically invading Tampa Bay more than the pirates are.

 

So, whether you're a Tampa local or just passing through on vacation, make sure to carve out some time for a coffee crawl. Because in the world of Tampa Bay caffeine, every sip is a journey, and every cup is a story waiting to be told. Sip happens, my friends, and it happens best in Tampa Bay!
